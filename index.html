About me

I caught the coding bug.

My name is Kelly Lloyd and I currently reside in St. Petersburg, FL. After graduating from the University of Florida in 2016 with a BA in English, I found myself working jobs in the real estate and legal fields. But I felt unsatisfied and underfulfilled walking down that career path, wanting to learn and work with something new, something exciting.

After I got laid off due to COVID-19, I gave coding a go. I completed online Python and JavaScript courses before transitioning into web development, where I found my calling. Over the past four years, I've devoted my time to learning and applying my knowledge to create various projects. When I got my first coding job, my passion for web development solidified and bloomed. I'm excited to work.../Web development allows me to incorporate my creativity and problem solving skills while working with new, emerging technologies.
